About us

Who are we?

We are an IT service provider. We provide help with any IT questions or any problems you might have with your laptop, network, security for your devices, and much more!

What makes us different?

What makes us different from everyother IT support or service companies? We have a staff that is always ready to help anyone who needs IT support. We don't have any waiting time for you to talk with a professional. Even when we are close we always have someone on stand by to troubleshoot any issues you might have.

Where do we service?

Our service area is all of northern indiana and goes as far south as Fort Wayne. This doesn't mean that if you don't live in this area, that you can't call us for help. Our team would be happy to help you no matter where you live. This only means if you don't live in this area we can't physically be there to install any cables or computers, etc. We have plans on making our service are bigger in time.
